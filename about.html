About

The inspiration

Hootparking is about contributing to help organize the city parking.

Bringing the community together, helping transform the frustration of parking to an enjoyable experience leaving them the chance the fully appreciate the city life.
Focus on the most important
From a local resident going to work, attending a meeting, rushing to conference or a new tourist hoping drive around, they can now focus on their destination.
Save on the rest
Spending less time looking for parking will allow users to save on gas, time, and be able to find the cheapest parking available nearby.
Protect our environment
Let's be green, taking a step toward reducing gas pollution in big cities.

Simplicity that works

Simplicity and beautiful design is our main focus to facilitate parking and make it an enjoyable experience. It's accessible to anyone and compatbile with the three major mobile platform devices (Apple, Android and Windows).

How it works?

  • Search
  • Park
  • Track
Users type the destination name, and the app will suggest the best option proving all the parking informations. Once arrived it will automatically mark your car as parked and will immediately notify other user about the occupancy of the parking spot. Then it will start keeping track of time and spendings to help user avoid parking tickets and surcharges.
